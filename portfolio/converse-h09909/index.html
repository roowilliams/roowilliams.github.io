Working with creative team Ben Dean and Linda Yang, I produced a prototype to test ability to detect light levels in a room via a user’s webcam and swap out content displayed accordingly.

Using this technology allowed someone to switch between a version of the video shot in light whilst watching in a lit room, and a darker, scarier version of the video by turning out the light.
