About

I started my career as a graphic designer in South Wales, UK, and then went on to study Product Design (industrial) at Central Saint Martins, London.

During 4 years of study I witnessed The Internet and proliferation of sensor-laden mobile devices disrupting industries around me, causing me to question if we, as a society, were heading towards dematerialization. Many of the problems I was trying to resolve with the design of physical objects seemed like they could be resolved by an app.

Within this tension between the physical and digital I discovered physical computing, and interaction design. Now I create things that live in both the physical and digital worlds using a multitude of technologies.

Currently living my best life in Brooklyn, New York with my partner Lydia, and our tiny dog Betty.
